Timeline of Unique

1983-Started as an agent

I started as an agent importing furniture to and from furniture stops all around the country while learning and studying from it.


1998-Opening Ceremony

After a certain period of study, the first Unique Furniture shop was founded on the 20th of November in 1983 and started selling furniture that was unique even for people at those times.

2005-Becoming more popular

The store became more and more well-known among people from one customer to another due to the uniqueness of our products, their reasonable prices and the services that our store provides.


2012-Establishment of 2 branches

Since then, the business has blossomed and by 2012 there are already 3 branches in 3 different townships all doing well with loyal customers.

2016-Establishment of the online shop

We started an online website of the Unique Furniture Shop so that customers can shop online and to expand our business.


What are we?


Our top priority and what makes us different from other stores are the services we provide for our dear customers. We have thought of the price and the style range of the furniture that would be both affordable and reasonable for any customer. We have thought of the delivery and the maintenance services for our customers' satisfactory and conveniency.
